## Globalization, Systems, and Data

I’m a Technical Program Manager focused on systems, data, and globalization.

Most of my work is in the gaps between teams: where product decisions meet real-world constraints, where data models break down in practice, and where global requirements surface issues that don’t show up in a single market. I spend a lot of time translating between perspectives—engineering, product, and customer-facing teams—so that decisions hold up under real usage, not just in design.

My background is in localization and international product development, including working directly with Japanese stakeholders and customers. That experience shaped how I approach program management: I assume misalignment exists until it’s proven otherwise, and I try to find and deal with it early, when it’s still fixable.

I’m particularly interested in roles that involve:
- distributed systems and data workflows  
- global product development  
- cross-functional programs with unclear ownership  
- situations where the “right” solution isn’t obvious at the start  

If that overlaps with what you’re working on, I’d be glad to talk.
